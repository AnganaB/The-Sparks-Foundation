Digital Constellation Program

The internet is not a luxury, it is a necessity.

- Barack Obama



About the program


Aims to have at least one person from every household to be computer literate.

Promotes computer literacy and e-learning.

To help the villagers aspire for better jobs and employment facilities.

Integration of digital technologies in everyday life.


Methodology


Digital Centers are set - up in the target areas.

If there is any school operational in the area, TSF collaborates with the school to get computer literacy as a mandatory curriculum for students.

Basic English language is taught to students to help them understand functions.

Digital Centers are open for Baithak, Sabal and TSF School projects.


Levels Of Literacy


Elementary literacy : Basic skills, MSOffice, internet banking and communication.

Functional literacy : Elementary literacy + social media + smart phones use.

Advanced literacy : Functional literacy + Basic programming skills.
