About the program


Ensures encouraged spirit, best career choices and continuous professional growth.

Aims to explore potential of mentor and mentee, both.


Activities


One-to-one mentor-mentee program.

Industry inspired workshops and conferences.

Specialization based seminars and guest lectures.

Career based inspiring talks and counselling programs.


Individual focused live sessions and problem solving mentorship.

Profession based certificate program tutored by industry experts.


Methodology


Collaboration with schools and colleges for academic mentorship and allocation of mentors based on psychological and behavioral study of mentees.

Registration through TSF website for non-academic and professional mentorship.

Periodic mentorship activities in schools, colleges, companies and webinars.
