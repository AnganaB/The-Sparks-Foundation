Educate a Village Program

It takes a village to raise a child.

- an old African saying



About the program


Partners adopt a village to raise literacy rate by at least 10% for a tenure.

Tenure may be renewed for the improvement of further 15% in literacy rate.

No more than two terms for a partner for a particular village.

Execution of activities is done by TSF members with the help of local authorities.


Methodology


Selection of a village from the profile database maintained by TSF.

Profile of villages contains information related to villages terms of literacy rate and educational infrastructure,demographics and ongoing government initiatives.

TSF and the partners collectively decide initiatives to be undertaken.

Multiple partners may collectively adopt a village depending on its backwardness.


Initiatives Involved


Baithak - Evening schools adults and senior citizens.

Sabal - Schools for house wives.

TSF Schools - Formal primary education platform for village children.
